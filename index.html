lastfm commands

set
Usage: >set <lastFMUsername>
Sets your Last.fm username with stormi.

delete
Alternate: reset
Usage: >delete
Deletes your Last.fm username from stormi.

sub
Usage: >sub
Subscribes you to the Weekly Recap. A personalized listening report sent to you every week.

unsub
Usage: >unsub
Unsubscribes you from the Weekly Recap. A personalized listening report sent to you every week.

info
Usage: >info or >info <lastFMUsername>
Returns information about a Last.fm profile.

np
Usage: >np or >np <lastFMUsername>
Returns the most recently listened track.

recent
Usage: >recent or >recent <lastFMUsername>
Returns the 10 most recently listened to tracks.

tracks
Usage: >tracks [period] or >tracks <period> <lastFMUsername>
Note: A period is optional and defaulted to all when fetching your own listening data. A period is required when a lastFMUsername is entered Returns the most listened to tracks for a given time period.

artists
Usage: >artists [period] or >artists <period> <lastFMUsername>
Note: A period is optional and defaulted to all when fetching your own listening data. A period is required when a lastFMUsername is entered.
Returns the most listened to artists for a given time period.

albums
Usage: >albums [period] or >albums <period> <lastFMUsername>
Note: A period is optional and defaulted to all when fetching your own listening data. A period is required when a lastFMUsername is entered.
Returns the most listened to albums for a given time period.

whoknows
Alias: wk

Usage: >wk or >wk <artistName>
Note: If an artistName isn't supplied it grabs information for your last scrobbled track.
Ranks the top listeners of an artist on the server.

wkalbum
Alias: wka

Usage: >wka or >wka <albumName>
Note: If an albumName isn't supplied it grabs information for your last scrobbled track.
Ranks the top listeners of an album on the server.

wktrack
Alias: wkt

Usage: >wkt or >wkt <trackName>
Note: If a trackName isn't supplied it grabs information for your last scrobbled track.
Ranks the top listeners of a track on the server.

cover
Usage: >cover or >cover <albumName>
Returns the album cover of your last listened track or of a searched album.

grid
Usage: >grid or >grid <gridSize> [period]

gridSize: 3x3 || 4x4 || 5x5

Note: A period is optional and defaulted to all when using the command with no arguments or just the gridSize
Returns a grid image of your top listened to album covers.

artist
Usage: >artist Doja Cat
Returns listening data, biography, and similar artists of any given artist.

topalbums
Usage: >topalbums Doja Cat
Returns the top albums of an artist sorted by popularity.

toptracks
Usage: >toptracks I Don't Do Drugs
Returns the top albums of an artist sorted by popularity.

serverinfo
Usage: >serverinfo
Returns basic information about the Discord server.

Administrator Only:

clear
Usage: >clear <numberOfMessages>
Clears a number of messages from the chat. Valid numberOfMessages is 1-100.
